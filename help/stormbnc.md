## What It Is
A Bouncer is a server which keeps you connected to IRC when you're away, and replays recent lines
when you reconnect.

## Signing Up
Ask in [#stormbnc on irc.stormbit.net](irc://irc.stormbit.net:6667/#stormbnc) to get a free ZNC
account!
